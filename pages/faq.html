FAQ

Answers to commonly asked questions

Orders & Shipping

How long does shipping take?

Domestic orders typically ship within 1-2 business days. Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days, expedited takes 2-3 days, and overnight shipping is available. See our shipping page for full details.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes! We ship to most countries worldwide. International shipping rates are calculated at checkout. Please note that customs duties and taxes may apply.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with tracking information. You can also log into your account to view order status and tracking details.

Returns & Exchanges

What's your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for unused items in original packaging. See our returns page for complete details and instructions.

What if I received a damaged item?

Contact us immediately with photos of the damage. We'll send a replacement right away at no additional cost to you.

Parts & Fitment

Will this part fit my car?

Each product listing includes fitment information. If you're unsure, contact us with your year, make, model, and trim level and we'll help verify fitment.

Do you offer installation?

We don't offer installation services directly, but we can recommend trusted shops in your area. Many of our parts are designed for DIY installation with basic tools.

Are the parts OEM or aftermarket?

We carry both! Product descriptions clearly indicate whether an item is OEM (original equipment manufacturer) or aftermarket. We only stock quality parts from trusted brands.

Merchandise

What sizes do you offer?

Apparel is available in sizes S through 3XL. Check individual product pages for size charts and specific measurements.

Can I exchange for a different size?

Absolutely! Contact us within 30 days and we'll arrange an exchange. Original item must be unworn with tags attached.

Payment & Security

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes! All transactions are encrypted with SSL technology. We never store your full credit card information on our servers.

Still Have Questions?

Can't find what you're looking for? Contact us and we'll be happy to help!
